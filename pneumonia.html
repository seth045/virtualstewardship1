Pneumonia

  • Non-severe Community Acquired Pneumonia (CAP)
  • Severe Community-acquired pneumonia
  • Pneumoniae with prior MRSA isolation within the past 90 days
  • Pneumoniae with prior Pseudomonas isolation within the past 90 days, Cystic Fibrosis, or Bronchiectasis
  • Acute aspiration of gastric contents. "Aspiration Pneumonitis"
  • Aspiration pneumonia
  • Aspiration pneumonia with anerobic risk
  • Hospital Acquired Pneumonia (HAP), Ventilator Associated Pneumonia (VAP)
  • HAP/VAP with IV antibiotic use in the previous 90 days, treatment in unit where MRSA prevalence is not known or >20%, or MRSA isolation within past 90 days
