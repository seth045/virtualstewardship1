Antimicrobial Guide

Community Acquired cholecystitis and cholangitis

Treatment

  • Escherichia coli
  • Klebsiella species
  • Bacteroides fragilis
  • Streptococcus species
  • Enterococcus species
  • Ceftriaxone + Metronidazole for 4 - 7 days from source control
  • Piperacillin/Tazobactam for 4 - 7 days from source control
